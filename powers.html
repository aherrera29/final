Satoru Gojo

"Infinity Exists Everywhere." -Satoru Gojo

Limitless (無下限, Mugen Kagen), is Satoru Gojo's signature cursed technique, allowing him to manipulate space at an atomic level. It provides him with a range of abilities centered around the concept of infinity. The core function of Limitless is to create an infinite barrier, which makes it impossible for any attack to physically touch him unless he permits it. Overall, Limitless grants Gojo unparalleled defensive and offensive capabilities, making him one of the most formidable characters in Jujutsu Kaisen.

Infinity (無限, Mugen): Creates an infinite barrier that slows down anything approaching him, making him untouchable.

Cursed Technique Lapse: Blue (術式順転「蒼」, Jutsushiki Junten: Ao): Generates a force of attraction, pulling objects toward a single point.

Cursed Technique Reversal: Red (術式反転「赫」, Jutsushiki Hanten: Aka): Creates a repulsive force that pushes everything away.

Hollow Purple (虚式「茈」, Kyoshiki: Murasaki): Combines Blue (attraction) and Red (repulsion) to erase matter from existence.

Six Eyes (六眼, Rokugan): Enhances Gojo's perception and cursed energy control, allowing him to use energy with near-perfect efficiency and perceive cursed energy in immense detail.

Domain Expansion: Unlimited Void (無量空処, Muryōkūsho): Traps opponents in a space where they are bombarded with infinite information, immobilizing them while Gojo can attack freely.
